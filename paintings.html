Light touches everything

Oil painting



Oil is great for capturing a sense of depth. Here's some shots of the details.

This painting explores how we are all connected. Light reflects from the buildings to our faces and back onto those around us. Similarly, our surroundings influence us and we influence them back. These interactions make life more interesting.




Body of emotions

Acrylic



This series explores how our body language convey emotions. Using the same model for each painting allowed me to experiment with many colors, techniques and perspectives.




Crowded

Acrylic, Watercolor, Ink



This series focuses on interactions within a crowd. There are a few pictures based on parties and celebrations, but being in a crowd can also be lonely at times.
