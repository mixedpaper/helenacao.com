Wateloo Banking Project


Better banking for students, by students




I am one of the founding directors of Waterloo Banking Project. I led the Education department, which organizes events to teach students how to manage their finances. I also worked on the branding, graphics and website.

The vision

We are a group of students who thinks banking should be more student-friendly.

We partnered with a credit union to offer student friendly products and help students get better at managing their finances. We believe that financial education should be part of the banking experience.

Pitching the idea

My co-founder and I pitched Waterloo Banking Project at OCE's Social Enterprise Student Competition and won a $20,000 grant to make our idea a reality. We were interviewed by CTV, CBC Radio, Fairchild TV and the university newspaper.

Financial education

Educating students about financial management is a big part of Waterloo Banking Project. I led the education department and hosted many events, helping over 300 students.
